Sheperds Pie

Description:

ENjoy the tastiest of the meat version so make sure you savour the dishes. Can get along well with bread and can also act as gravy.

ingredients:

  • 4 medium potatoes
  • 1 cut of cauliflower
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1 cube of chicken stock or veggie stock
  • 1/4 cup of warm water
  • 1 chicken breast
  • 1/2 cup chopped carrots,onions
  • 1 tomoato
  • a cup of cheddar cheese

Directions:

  1. Step1:

    Bring a pot of water to a boil; add the potatoes and cauliflower and cook at a boil until soft, about 20 minutes. Drain and transfer to a large bowl. Pour the milk over the mixture and mash with a potato masher until smooth.

  2. Step2:

    Crumble the beef bouillon cube into the water; whisk until the bouillon is dissolved. Pour into a slow cooker. Add the stew meat, bacon, carrot, onion, garlic, and tomato to the slow cooker.

  3. Step3:

    Spread the mashed cauliflower and potato mixture over the meat and vegetable mixture. Sprinkle the Cheddar cheese over the top.

  4. Step4:

    Set the slow cooker to Low and cook for 8 hours, or, if you prefer, at High for 4 hours. Take it out and Voilla!!
