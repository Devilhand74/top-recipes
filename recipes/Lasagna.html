Lasagna

Description:

WHo doesnt like this bomb dish!??? EVeryone loves it and everyone wants a piece of it. Of course theres one for everyone and here comes the super easy recipe to make one. Of course all you need are the following ingredients.

ingredients

  • 1 ounce lasagna noodles
  • 1 kg chicken or beef
  • salt and pepper
  • Spaghetti sauce(1tsp)
  • cloves(minced)
  • 1/2kg mozzarella cheese
  • cheddar cheese
  • ricotta cheese(1pint)

Directions:

  1. Step1:

    Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.

  2. Step2:

    Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). In a large skillet over medium-high heat, brown beef and season with salt and pepper; drain. Stir in spaghetti sauce and garlic and simmer 5 minutes.

  3. Step3:

    In a medium bowl, combine mozzarella, Cheddar and ricotta; stir well. In 9x13 inch pan, alternate layers of noodles, meat mixture and cheese mixture until pan is filled.

  4. Step4:

    Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly. NOw pull out and Take in the first bite of this delicious piece!!
